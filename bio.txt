= Matt Sparks: The Life and Times =

{{http://lh3.ggpht.com/_SxQbjXQwvSs/Sd28jN1lZzI/AAAAAAAABlE/UbF8wl3ssEc/s800/IMG_1833.JPG?200|_Matt Sparks}}

[[http://quadpoint.org|Matt Sparks]] received his first dose of artificial
light in a hospital in Marion, Illinois in early
[[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1986|1986]]. He lived in Marion until age five,
when he made the journey to his so-called hometown of Charleston, Illinois
(home of [[http://www.eiu.edu|EIU]]). Matt spent his elementary years in
Charleston. Partly into the freshman year at
[[http://www.charleston.k12.il.us/CHS|Charleston High School]], he moved to the
Springfield, Illinois area. Matt finished off his high school career in Chatham
at [[http://ghsonline.net|Glenwood High School]] with the class of 2004.

In December 2008, he completed his B.S. in [[http://www.cs.uiuc.edu|Computer
Science]] in the [[http://www.engr.uiuc.edu|College of Engineering]] at the
[[http://www.uiuc.edu|University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign]] (UIUC).

Matt now has a master's degree in [[http://cs.stanford.edu|Computer Science]] from [[http://stanford.edu|Stanford University]], completed in June 2011.
